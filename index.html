About

deSolidState has been creating digital artifacts since 2000.

currently fascinated with the exponential increase of video screens that we see moment to moment, deSolidState experiments on the margins of the current video grammar creating post-ambient abstractions that examine the ethical implications of our technological present by exploring the technical and metaphoric limitations of various media as they pass into obsolescence.

in other guises deSolidState creates woodcarvings, installations and other conversation-driven art.
